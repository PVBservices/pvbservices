Projects

Hardware

PVB consulted over 10 projects in hardware design and prototyping in Belgium, France, Portugal, Spain, and UK. These projects include box design for cyber-physical systems such as data loggers, water and electricity metering sensors, gaming interfaces, network rooters, etc. Moreover, we consulted our partners to develop IoT solutions using Arduino, Bluetooth, WIFI, and LoRaWAN.

Software

PVB has successfully developed front-end and back-end software for its clients mainly in Belgium, Portugal, and UK. These projects are carried out using Java, Python, Angular, Hadoop Cluster, Sparks and other cutting edge big data, back-end, and UI development stacks.
